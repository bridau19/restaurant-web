Jiyun's

home-cooked american breakfast

Jiyun's is a friendly and inviting place to enjoy a delicious American breakfast cooked with love from a Korean American's perspective.

Growing up in California, I always felt like a typical American girl, except for when my mother would cook breakfast. She always cooked traditional American breakfast foods, but added in dishes and techniques she remembered from growing up in Korea and later living in a Korean household in America.

Eating mom's food made me feel both connected to my heritage and part of America at the same time. She showed me that being American does not mean forgetting where you came from, but embracing the many cultures and traditions that live in a nation of immigrants. . . uh, sort of

Now Jiyun's brings you the best of both worlds, with warm meals to fill you up and make you smile!
